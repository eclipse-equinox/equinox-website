Archived material

The Equinox incubator has evolved from its original investigation into runtime alternatives through various other work areas. As work areas are promoted to the production codebase or abandoned, their work material, documents and web site content are archived here.

  • Phase 1 - content and repo tar. Phase 1 of Equinox was the orirginal runtime and dynamic plug-in work. After Eclipse 3.0 shipped, Phase 1 was terminated and Phase 2 started. These archives include both the resource history (i.e., a tar of the content in the repo) and a snapshot of the content at the time of archiving (i.e., a zip of the contents of HEAD).
  • Dynamic plug-ins - This work carried on into Phase 2 but was largely incorporated in Eclipse 3.1.
