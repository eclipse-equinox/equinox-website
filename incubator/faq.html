Frequently Asked Questions
Q.

I thought Equinox shipped their stuff. Is the project dead?
The runtime developed as part of Equinox was in fact shipped as part of Eclipse 3.0. There are still several open questions related to the Eclipse runtime however and work in the Equinox project is continuing.
Q.

What is Phase 2 of Equinox?
The first main thrust of Equinox was to produce a new runtime for Eclipse. This was completed with the release of Eclipse 3.0. Phase 2 collectively refers to the work in Equinox which started after Eclipse 3.0 was released (June 2004).
Q.

What is the relationship between Equinox and the Eclipse platform project?
The Eclipse platform project is the main development effort for Eclipse. Equinox is a forum for exploring approaches and technologies which could be used by the platform to increase its runtime flexibility.
Q.

What does Equinox deliver/ship?
The Equinox project is designed to provide input to the Eclipse platform effort. As such, it delivers well-thoughtout prototype solutions to particular issues. While it may offer interrim downloads of its work in progress, Equinox will not support ongoing, stand-alone function which has not/will not be rolled into the platform proper.
Q.

Why isn't this work being done in the Eclipse platform project?
There are two main reasons:

  1. Equinox is exploration. Some of the work will bear fruit and some will not. Some of the work is long term and may not make it into the next release.
  2. On the purely pragmatic side, having a separate project provides the infrastructure and support for a safe and productive workarea.

Keeping Equinox separate reduces risk and confusion. Having said that, it is fundamental to the approach that Equinox have strong participation from the platform project members.
Q.

Who can/should participate in Equinox?
Equinox needs people who:

  • have real requirements to run Eclipse in scenarios which depart from that seen in standard tooling situations
  • have a strong desire to dig into the Eclipse runtime model
  • are willing and able to contribute production quality code
Q.

Will Equinox grow/change over time?
Yes and No. We are purposely starting small. If the working model is successful, we will proceed to address more of the aspects scoped out in the Equinox description. Having said that, the overall focus and direction of Equinox will not change. People wanting to use the "technology project approach" in other areas should seek to spawn other technology projects rather than joining Equinox.
